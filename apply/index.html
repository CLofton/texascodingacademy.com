Application Process
Interested in taking the next steps?
The acceptance process only takes 30 minutes!

1. Submit Application
Join Texas's next generation of developers. Get started with our quick and easy questionnaire.

2. Phone Interview
We'd like to get to know you better, understand your technical skill level, and answer any questions you have.

3. Admissions Assignment
We assign a short admissions assignment composed of an essay and coding challenge. These are designed to gauge applicant's goals and work ethic more than their technical expertise.

4. Enrollment
Remote classes fill up fast and we want to make sure students can get started on their pre-work quickly.

5. Pre-work
Once you're enrolled in class, you'll have some pre-work to complete before the start of term.

Ready to apply?
After you complete the application, you will be prompted to schedule a 15 minute Admissions Phone Interview. Congrats on taking the first step to a new career in web development!
